Leonardo DiCaprio Refuses to Reveal Who He Was Talking to in Viral Golden Globes Moment

Leonardo DiCaprio photo by Shoot for the Stars, CC BY 4.0
2026-01-23T13:44:44

Leonardo DiCaprio is keeping the mystery alive when it comes to his viral moment at the 2026 Golden Globes.

The actor was caught on camera laughing and making animated facial expressions while speaking to someone seated just out of view during the awards ceremony.

The clip was shared on social media by the Golden Globes and quickly spread online, prompting widespread speculation about who DiCaprio was speaking with.

Following the viral moment, DiCaprio earned his eighth Oscar nomination for his performance in One Battle After Another.

Shortly after the nomination announcement, the 51-year-old actor addressed the viral clip in an interview.

He confirmed that he was talking to a friend but declined to share any names.

DiCaprio joked that he didn't know whether the other person would even want to be mentioned publicly.

Referencing a line from A Bronx Tale, the actor quipped that he is "not a rat."

Speculation briefly centered on his One Battle After Another costar Teyana Taylor.

Taylor initially suggested they were chatting about how her children love the animated film KPop Demon Hunters.

She later clarified that DiCaprio may have been speaking to someone else during the viral moment.

Taylor joked that DiCaprio seemed to be having multiple enthusiastic conversations about the same topic throughout the night.

During a commercial break, DiCaprio was seen pointing toward the unseen person and laughing as he spoke.

The individual he was addressing did not appear in the video clip shared online.

One Battle After Another had a strong night during awards season.

The film won multiple Golden Globes and received several major nominations across awards bodies.

DiCaprio praised director Paul Thomas Anderson for the film's depth and political relevance.

He described the movie as original filmmaking that reflects the current state of the world.

The Oscar nomination places DiCaprio among a select group of actors with seven or more Academy Award nominations.

Despite the attention surrounding the viral clip, DiCaprio appears content to keep the identity of his conversation partner private.
